Development Blog

Week 1

  • 18 OCT, 2022

During our first week, we dedicated ourselves to thoroughly researching the project and gathering all necessary information about our target users. To do this, we conducted a series of observations and interviews over the course of the week to gather insights and develop a clear understanding of the people who would be using our product.

Based on the information we gathered, we then created personas that represent the various types of users who would be utilizing our product. This exercise allowed us to get a deeper understanding of the project and the specific needs and requirements of our target users. By having this information, we were able to design our prototype in a way that was tailored specifically to meet the needs of the people who would be using it.

Overall, our research efforts during week one helped us to lay the foundation for our project and set us up for success moving forward. We gained knowledge about our target audience and the requirements they have, which will be essential as we move forward with developing and refining our prototype.

Week 2

  • 25 OCT, 2022

During our second week, we turned our attention to developing the prototype for our project. With the information we gathered about user requirements and the progress made on our HCI report, we had a much clearer understanding of the direction we wanted to take with our project.

To bring our ideas to life, we used Figma, a powerful and user-friendly collaborative software. With Figma, we were able to create a wireframe representation of our prototype, which gave us a visual representation of how our project would function and look. This was an essential step in the development process as it allowed us to refine our ideas and make any necessary changes before moving forward with the next stages of development.

Overall, our focus during week two was all about bringing our prototype to life. By utilizing Figma and creating a wireframe representation of our project, we were able to take significant steps forward in the development process and bring our ideas one step closer to becoming a reality.

Week 3

  • 1 Nov, 2022

During week three of our project, our main objective was to make sure that our prototype and HCI report were of the highest quality before submission.

Once our prototype and HCI report were finalized and submitted, we turned our attention to beginning work on our literature review. Our literature review was focused on researching Architectural CAD walkthroughs and PseudoVR. This research was crucial in helping us determine the most appropriate CAD software for our study, which turned out to be SketchUp and AutoCAD.

Working on this literature review allowed us to dive deeper into the understanding of our project and gain a better understanding of how we should approach its implementation. By conducting this research, we were able to identify the key factors that would impact the success of our project and develop a plan for moving forward that was informed by the best practices and principles of our field.

Week 4

  • 8 Nov, 2022

During week four of our project, we recognized the importance of staying organized and focusing on the administrative side of things.

To accomplish this, we began building the project website and uploading all necessary information and documentation. This included our HCI report, literature review, prototype, and any other relevant materials. Having all of this information in one central location was essential in helping us stay organized and informed throughout the project.

Another key aspect of staying on track was making sure that we were following our project plan. To accomplish this, we created a Gantt chart which helped us to monitor our progress and allocate time efficiently. With this, we were able to see exactly where we stood in relation to our project timeline, and make any necessary adjustments to keep us on track.

Week 5 + Week 6

  • 15-22Nov,2022

During the development phase of our project website, we recognized the importance of familiarizing ourselves with the SketchUp and AutoCAD software. This was necessary as we needed to be able to effectively navigate the workspace and perform various functions. To ensure that we had a solid understanding of these programs, we took the time to make a list of all the commands used for each feature. This allowed us to implement the functionality for each feature separately and efficiently.

In addition to familiarizing ourselves with SketchUp and AutoCAD, we also spent time getting familiar with the MotionInput source code. This was a completely new area for us, and it was crucial that we understood how it worked in order to make progress on our project. By taking the time to understand these programs, this ensured we were able to begin the designing and implementation process.

Week 6

  • 15 Nov, 2022

We have updated the speech recognition system so that it's vocabulary be limited for certain software. This is beneficial as it allows for speech commands to be triggered reliably, especially in the case of some difficult words such as "orbit" which often got confused for the phrase "or bit".

This was achieved by passing a JSON list of acceptable words to the KaldiRecognizer object.
